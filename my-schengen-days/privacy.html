Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Overview

My Schengen Days ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

My Schengen Days is designed with privacy in mind. All your trip data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Data Storage

All information you enter into the app, including:

  • Trip dates
  • Trip notes
  • Calculation history

This data is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. We have no access to this information.

Third-Party Services

My Schengen Days does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, its security is managed by your device's built-in security features. We recommend using device passcode or biometric authentication to protect your data.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
