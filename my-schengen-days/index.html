About the App

My Schengen Days helps travelers track their stays in the Schengen zone to ensure compliance with the 90/180 day rule. Never worry about overstaying again!

  • Track all your trips automatically
  • See remaining days at a glance
  • Get warnings before you exceed limits
  • Works offline - your data stays private
